Red Pesto Pasta

Sufficiently filling and indulgent, this pasta dish is quick and easy too!

Pesto pasta is typically served with in the green variant, but this recipe will be covering the red version.

The best part about pesto pasta is that it can be made, start to finish, in roughly 20 minutes or less. This recipe will be made with dried pasta.

Ingredients

  • 250g Dry Pasta (of choice)
  • 190g Sun Dried Tomato Pesto
  • 100ml Double Cream
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Optional Hard Cheese

Step by Step Process

  1. Fill a saucepan with water half way, salt it and bring to the boil.
  2. Place your chosen pasta into the water and set a timer for 10 minutes.
  3. Drizzle a teaspoon of olive oil into the water to stop the pasta from sticking.
  4. Drain pasta at 10 minute mark, bring heat to low and place pasta back in.
  5. Add your pesto sauce into the pan and stir through, ensuring all pasta is coated.
  6. Pour in your cream slowly while stirring to create an indulgent sauce.
  7. Grind black pepper over the pasta to your taste.
  8. Plate up and add more black pepper and dust with chosen hard cheese.
