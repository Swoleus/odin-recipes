Red Pesto Pasta

Sufficiently filling and indulgent, this pasta dish is quick and easy too!

Pesto pasta is typically served with in the green variant, but this recipe will be covering the red version.

The best part about pesto pasta is that it can be made, start to finish, in roughly 20 minutes or less. This recipe will be made with dried pasta.

Ingredients

  • 250g Dry Pasta (of choice)
  • 190g Sun Dried Tomato Pesto
  • 100ml Double Cream
  • Optional Hard Cheese
  • Black Pepper
