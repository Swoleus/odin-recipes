Air Fryer Chicken Wings

Tender and crispy chicken wings made in the air fryer!

A timely and cost-effecient means to creating perfectly crispy and succulent chicken wings every time.

Serve with anything you like as well, be it a salad, some chips or even by themselves.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Wings
  • 50g Garlic Seasoning
  • 50g All Purpose Chicken Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper and Salt

Step by Step Process

  1. Wash the chicken, dry it, then add seasonings with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat airfryer on "Steam Airfry" mode set at 220 degrees celsius.
  3. Place chicken into air fryer, add 100ml of water and 50ml of oil.
  4. Set timer for 23 minutes, checking every 5 and flipping at 10 minute intervals.
  5. Once crisped to your liking, remove chicken and leave to stand for 3 minutes.
  6. Plate up chicken with any choice of sides and add some more black pepper to garnish.
