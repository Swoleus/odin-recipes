Red Pesto Pasta

Tender and crispy chicken wings made in the air fryer!

A timely and cost-effecient means to creating perfectly crispy and succulent chicken wings every time.

Serve with anything you like as well, be it a salad, some chips or even by themselves.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Wings
  • 50g Garlic Seasoning
  • 50g All Purpose Chicken Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Black Pepper and Salt

Step by Step Process

  1. Wash the chicken, dry it, then add seasonings with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat airfryer on
  3. Drizzle a teaspoon of olive oil into the water to stop the pasta from sticking.
  4. Drain pasta at 10 minute mark, bring heat to low and place pasta back in.
  5. Add your pesto sauce into the pan and stir through, ensuring all pasta is coated.
  6. Pour in your cream slowly while stirring to create an indulgent sauce.
  7. Grind black pepper over the pasta to your taste.
  8. Plate up and add more black pepper and dust with chosen hard cheese.
