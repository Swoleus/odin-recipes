Bacon Sandwich

The Classic One Size Fits All Sandwich

Less is more with this recipe, though it's foundation can be built upon for anyone's specific taste.

All you'll need is bread, bacon and butter.

Ingredients

  • 300g Smoked or Unsmoked Back Bacon
  • 50g Salted Butter
  • Bread of Choice

Step by Step Process

  1. Drop your 50g of butter into a frying pan and bring the temperature up to a medium-high.
  2. Once hot, place bacon strips into pan and ensure to flip every 60 seconds.
  3. Once bacon is at desired crispiness, place in a tissue lined bowel to absorb excess fat.
  4. Place bread slices into pan for 60 seconds to crisp up in the bacon grease.
  5. Assemble sandwich and add whatever you wish into it. Brown sauce is a great addition.
